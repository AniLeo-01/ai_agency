Build a SaaS team workspace tool for remote-first startups (10-50 people).

The core problem: remote teams waste hours every week switching between Slack, Notion, Jira, and Google Docs. Context gets lost, decisions are buried in threads, and new hires take weeks to find anything.

What we want:
- A unified workspace where each project has its own space with docs, tasks, and discussions in one place
- Real-time collaborative documents (like Notion but simpler — just headings, text, checklists, and embedded images)
- A Kanban-style task board per project with assignees, due dates, priorities, and labels
- A threaded discussion feed per project (not a chat — more like async updates and decisions)
- A global search that searches across all docs, tasks, and discussions
- An activity feed on the home page showing what changed across all your projects
- User roles: workspace owner, admin, member, guest (view-only)
- SSO with Google and GitHub for onboarding
- Notifications: in-app and email digest (daily/weekly configurable)

Nice to have:
- Slash commands in docs to embed tasks or reference other docs
- A "decisions log" per project that auto-collects messages marked as decisions
- Weekly auto-generated summary email per project

Target users are startup founders, engineering leads, and product managers who are tired of tool sprawl. We want to charge $12/user/month with a free tier for up to 5 users.
