We're building a niche e-commerce marketplace for independent furniture makers and artisan craftspeople to sell directly to consumers.

Background: platforms like Etsy have become oversaturated with mass-produced dropshipped goods. Real artisans are getting buried. We want to build a curated marketplace where every seller is vetted and every piece is genuinely handmade.

Seller side:
- Application and vetting process: sellers submit a portfolio with photos, a description of their craft, and their workshop. Our team reviews and approves/rejects.
- Seller dashboard: manage products (photos, description, dimensions, materials, price, shipping options, lead time), view orders, track revenue, respond to buyer messages.
- Product listings support: up to 10 photos per product, a video embed, material tags, customization options (e.g., "choose wood finish: oak/walnut/cherry"), and made-to-order flag with estimated lead time.
- Shipping integration: sellers enter package dimensions/weight, and we auto-calculate shipping via integration with major carriers. Support both domestic and international.

Buyer side:
- Browse by category (tables, chairs, lighting, decor, etc.), by material, by price range, by lead time, or by maker
- Product pages with high-res image gallery, zoom, maker bio, reviews, and "ask the maker" messaging
- Save favorites / wishlist
- Cart and checkout with Stripe payments. Support guest checkout.
- Order tracking: from "order placed" to "crafting" to "shipped" to "delivered"
- Reviews and ratings after delivery (with photo uploads)

Platform:
- We take a 12% commission on each sale
- Featured maker spotlights on the homepage (editorial content)
- SEO-optimized product and maker pages
- Admin dashboard: manage sellers (approve/suspend), moderate reviews, view platform-wide analytics (GMV, orders, new sellers, conversion rate)
- Dispute resolution flow for damaged items or non-delivery

This is web-first but the buyer experience must be excellent on mobile browsers. Native apps can come later.
